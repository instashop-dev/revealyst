## All three tracked findings are now fixed

**1. E2E harness now runs the production connector + engine.** `seams.ts` resolves the real
`anthropicConsoleConnector` and the real `recomputeOrg`; obsolete reference stubs deleted.

**2. Ratio-component fabricated-zero bug** (`src/scoring/evaluate.ts`). A ratio component
with either side absent no longer floors to a fabricated `raw: 0` — it's omitted from the
breakdown entirely. 3 regression tests added.

**3. Stale person-level score rows on identity relink** (`src/scoring/recompute.ts`,
`src/db/org-scope.ts`). Previously `upsertResults` only inserted/updated — a person who
stopped being exclusively linked to a subject (relinked into a shared account, or dropped to
zero signal) kept their last-computed row forever, permanently disconnected from current
attribution. Fixed: a new `scores.deleteStalePersonResults(definitionId, period,
keepPersonIds)` reconciles person-level `score_results` for one (definition, period) down to
exactly the people that round's recompute actually scored — called once per person-level
definition in `recomputeOrg`, right after that round's upserts are known. Idempotent (safe
to re-run; nothing left to remove converges to 0) and scoped tightly (only touches
person-level rows for the exact definition+period being recomputed, never other definitions,
periods, or team-level rows). `RecomputeSummary` now surfaces `stalePersonResultsRemoved` —
reconciliation is counted and visible, never silent. 2 regression tests added, reproducing
the exact scenario from the adversarial finding (an exclusive subject relinked to a second
person mid-history) plus an idempotency check.

Scope note: this fix is intentionally person-level only, matching the confirmed finding and
its W2-K identity-mapping trigger. Team-level rows for a team that keeps existing but loses
all members are a structurally similar but *distinct*, not-yet-confirmed risk — not fixed
here; flag separately if found live.
